About Me

In 2024 I graduated from Brigham Young University with a degree in Computer Engineering. I've enjoyed working in a variety of fields such as web development, integrated systems, analog circuits, and game development. Currently, I'm working as a technical artist and programmer at Red Team Interactive, a growing indie game studio. I love learning new and efficient ways to create enjoyable and fun experiences for our users.

I am passionate about learning everything that I can to impact the world to be healthier, happier, and more efficient. Among my greatest goals is my desire to create higher educational opportunties for individuals in impoverished and low-opportunity communities. I hold myself to integrity, understanding, and an open mind in order to communicate efficiently to my coworkers, peers, friends, and family.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, coding, learning, reading books, and spending time with my wonderful wife. I'm currently playing through each of the Dark Souls games, after getting hooked on Elden Ring - fun! Project Hail Mary is the best book that I've read recently and I'm excited to watch the movie. My wife and I enjoy playing tennis, walking in nature, and watching shows with our cat.
