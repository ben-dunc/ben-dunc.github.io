BYU Engineering Leadership Certificate

The BYU Engineering Leadership Certificate is a certificate award by the BYU Engineering College to students who complete a set of requirements designed to increase the leadership abilites of students. The requirements were:

  • Read 3 books on leadership and how to apply their principles to leadership.
  • Participate in two leadership experiences of 30 or more hours within a class, club, or job and present the lessons learned to the ELC committee.
I was able to complete these requirements before graduating, receiving the ELC award at my graduating ceremony.
